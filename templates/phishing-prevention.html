Red Flags Checklist

Use these indicators as a mental “tap target” checklist. Hover over the suspicious link line and compare.

warning
Urgent or threatening language ("verify immediately", "account suspended").
language
Sender domain doesn't match the real company domain.
person
Generic greeting ("Dear Customer").
link
Suspicious link (hover to see the real destination; compare with displayed text).
attachment
Unexpected attachments or link "re-entry" to log in again.
key
Requests for sensitive information (passwords, MFA codes, payment details).
